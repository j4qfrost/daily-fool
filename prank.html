Vince Garin Dies in Tragic Car Accident

By John Doe | April 1, 2023

Vince Garin, a well-known community leader and founder of the St. Louis Youth Sports Association, died in a car accident on Thursday evening. The accident occurred on I-64 near Clayton Road, when Garin's car collided with a semi-truck.

Garin, 27, was known for his dedication to improving the lives of young people in the St. Louis area. He founded the St. Louis Youth Sports Association in 2005, and since then the organization has grown to serve over 1,000 children each year.

"Vince was an incredible person who touched the lives of so many people in our community," said St. Louis Mayor Jane Smith. "He will be deeply missed."

The cause of the accident is currently under investigation. Anyone with information is asked to contact the St. Louis Police Department.
